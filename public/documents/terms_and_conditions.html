Security Rules and Campus Code of Conduct

Valuables

If Delegates must bring valuables to the conference, they are encouraged to place them in a safe deposit box or other safe storage space at the hotel they are staying in. HPAIR and Harvard University are not responsible for belongings left in event rooms.

Refund Policy

You understand that refunds or other consideration will not be granted for any reason except as outlined in HPAIR policy and procedure, and agree that you may be expelled from the conference at any time for violating this agreement with no expectation of refund. You further understand that $150 of my registration fee will be construed as a non-refundable deposit. Participants may receive credit that will carry over to conferences and HPAIR events in the future. By submitting registration to the conference, you agree to the Refund Policy as described above.
